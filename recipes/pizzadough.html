Pizza, Dough to Dish

Ingredients

For The Dough

  • Pizza Flour
  • Dried Yeast
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Water

For The Toppings

  • Tin Chopped Tomatos
  • Parsley
  • Basil
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Toppings Of Choice

Method

Starter

  1. Start with 300ml of water in a larger tupperware tub. Mix in 5g of dried yeast. Once mixed, add 5g of honey and mix again. Finally add 300g of pizza flour and mix until everything is combined.
  2. Place lig on tubberware and leave at room temperature for 20 minutes. After 20 minutes place in the fridge for 24 hours to allow the yeast to ferment.
