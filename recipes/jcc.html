Jamacian Curried Chicken

Ingredients

  • Curried Goat Spices
  • Chicken (chunks)
  • Garlic (crushed)
  • Scotch Bonnet (diced)
  • Onion (diced)
  • Potatos (diced)
  • Green/Red Pepper (diced)
  • Carrot (coins)
  • Ground All Spice
  • Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • 1.5L veg/chicken stock

Method

  1. Combine chicken, scotch bonnet, garlic, onions, peppers and carrots into a bowl then add 1.5 tbsp of curried goat spices and 1 tspn of ground all spice. Season with salt and black pepper and mix until everything is coated. Cover and place in the fridge for 30 minutes to marinade.
  2. Once your chicken has marinated. Heat a small amount of oil in a pan and mix a tbsp of curry spices with the oil. Once the oil has heated add the chicken and veg to the pan to cook the chicken through. Stir occationally to prevent burning.
  3. After 10-15 minutes the chicken should be cooked. Now add potatos and stir until coated with the spices. Then add your stock, bring it to a simmering temperature and leave for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve with rice.
