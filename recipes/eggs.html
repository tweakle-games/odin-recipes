Egg's ala`Dawn

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Method

  1. Crack eggs into bowl or measuring jug and whisk until combined.
  2. Add salt, pepper, a splash of milk and a small amount of grated cheese to the misture.
  3. Heat olive oil in an appropriately sized frying pan on a low heat and add egg mixture
  4. Stir occasionaly to begin with, lifting cooked pieces of egg from the bottom but becareful not to stir to much and break up the cooked pieces.
  5. The cooking process should take roughly 2-3 minutes. Stiring gently and removing fro heat once all the liquid has solidified.
  6. Serve on toast or with a full english breakfast. Perfect for egg fried rice.
